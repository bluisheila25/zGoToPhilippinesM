Boracay



Where to stay

Experience an ultimate luxury in travel, the best way to enjoy your holiday to the fullest is by visiting Boracay. Home of the famous White Sand beaches and water sports adventures, relax among the finest hotels and resorts in Boracay from Station 1 to Station 3.


Find Landmarks

Take a sightseeing getaway or a trip to Boracay historical landmarks and famous attractions such as the enchanting coves and turquoise.
  


Find Adventure

Day or night, there is no better way to beat the tropical heat than having extreme Boracay marine and wildlife adventures.


Find Nightlife

Get your own slice of pleasure and paradise. Visually stunning and perfect nightlife activities can only be found in Boracay.


Find Wellness

There are numerous places to get a good massage. Some of the bigger hotels have an in-house massage service and offers erotic massage therapy.


Where to Dine

Once in Boracay, you can never go wrong in choosing the right dining place for you along with the nature themed entertainment surrounding each place.


Where to Party

Have a weekend-long festival of street parties, dancing, food and festivities. Area bars are getting in on the fun as well, offering drink specials and more. There are numerous spots to party in Boracay.
